Cascade Mountain Range Temperature Comparator

Inversions occur when temperatures at higher elevations rise above the temperatures of lower elevations.
These phenomena are rare and are not easily tracked by any systems currently. With this visualizaion, you can easily
determine where an inversion event has occurred by taking note of where the higher elevation temperatures rise above
the lower elevation temperatures. These are found as the darkest saturation areas on each graph.
